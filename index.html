Our Games
Bullet Simulator — is an unusual shooter where you control a bullet right in the air! Slow down or speed up the movement, go around obstacles and defeat enemies. With each level it becomes more difficult: more enemies, more rooms, more tactics. Upgrade bullets and weapons, improve their speed, quantity, penetrating ability and aerodynamics. Unlock new types of weapons - from a pistol to a sniper rifle - and customize them to suit your playing style. Plan each shot, save ammo and watch who you aim at. In this game, every hit counts!
